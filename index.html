Our Profile

Connecting Markets

Based in Dubovo, Raski, Gaconi Design DOO operates as a merchant wholesaler of nondurable goods. While our name speaks to "Design," our core strength lies in the logistics and supply chain management required to move products efficiently.

Under the leadership of Munir Mujanovic, we have established a reputation for reliability in the Serbian wholesale sector. We specialize in identifying high-demand nondurable goods and ensuring they reach the market with speed and precision.

We believe that successful wholesale trade requires more than just storage; it requires a keen eye for market trends and a commitment to operational excellence.
